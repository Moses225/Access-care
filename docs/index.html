AccessCare

Connecting SoonerCare & Medicaid Patients with Oklahoma Healthcare Providers

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

The Short Version (Plain English)

  • We collect only what we need to help you find and book healthcare appointments.
  • We never sell your data to anyone — ever.
  • We never share your information with advertisers.
  • You can delete your account and all your data at any time.
  • Your appointment information is stored securely on Google's Firebase platform.

1. Who We Are

AccessCare is a mobile application designed to help SoonerCare and Medicaid patients in Oklahoma find and connect with healthcare providers who accept their insurance. AccessCare is operated by Moise Kouassi ("we," "us," or "our").

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you have questions, please contact us at the address listed at the bottom of this document.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Booking information: Your name and phone number when you request a healthcare appointment.
  • Provider information (for healthcare providers): Your name, specialty, practice address, phone number, office hours, accepted insurance plans, and profile photo when you set up a provider profile.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Which screens you visit and features you use within the app, to help us improve the experience.
  • Device information: Basic device type and operating system version for troubleshooting.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your Social Security Number, Medicaid ID number, or any government-issued ID.
  • We do not collect your medical records, diagnoses, or treatment history.
  • We do not collect payment or financial information.
  • We do not access your phone contacts, camera, or microphone unless you explicitly grant permission for a specific feature.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Connect you with healthcare providers who accept SoonerCare and Medicaid
  • Send appointment confirmation and status notifications via SMS
  • Allow healthcare providers to manage their availability and respond to appointment requests
  • Improve the app's features and fix technical issues
  • Respond to your questions or support requests
Important: We do not use your information for advertising. We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. AccessCare does not display advertisements.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your data is stored on Google Firebase, a secure cloud platform operated by Google LLC. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest.

We use Firebase's security rules to ensure that:

  • Patients can only see their own appointment records — not other patients' data.
  • Healthcare providers can only see appointment requests made to them specifically.
  • No user can access or modify another user's account information.

5. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your healthcare provider: When you request an appointment, the provider receives your name and phone number.
  • With service providers: We use Google Firebase to store data. These providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.
  • When required by law: We may disclose information if required by a valid legal process or court order.
  • With your consent: We will share information in other circumstances only with your explicit permission.

6. Children's Privacy

AccessCare is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access your data: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct your data: Update or correct inaccurate information through the app's profile settings.
  • Delete your data: Request deletion of your account and all associated personal information within 30 days.
  • Opt out of SMS: Reply STOP to any SMS message from AccessCare.

8. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Appointment records are retained for up to 12 months. When you delete your account, we delete your personal information within 30 days.

9. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — data storage and authentication
  • Google Maps — provider location display
  • Expo / EAS — app delivery and updates

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" at the top reflects when the policy was last updated. Continued use of AccessCare after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
