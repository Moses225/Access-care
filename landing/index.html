Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Morava

How do I find a SoonerCare doctor in Oklahoma City? ▾

Use Morava's free provider search above. Search by specialty or city, and all listed providers accept SoonerCare and Medicaid patients. Download the Morava app to book an appointment directly.

Is Morava really free for patients? ▾

Yes — Morava is completely free for every patient, forever. No subscription, no hidden fees, no paywalls. We never charge patients to search or book appointments.

Does Morava work if I'm uninsured? ▾

Absolutely. Many providers in our network offer sliding-scale fees and accept uninsured patients. You can filter by "uninsured" or "self-pay" in the Morava app to find providers who welcome cash-pay patients.

What insurance plans does Morava support? ▾

Morava supports all major insurance plans including SoonerCare, Medicaid, Medicare, BlueCross BlueShield, Aetna, United Healthcare, Cigna, Humana, Tricare, CHIP, and most employer plans. Uninsured patients are also welcome.

How do I book an appointment through Morava? ▾

Download the free Morava app on Android, create a free account, search for a provider, and tap "Book Appointment." You'll receive an SMS confirmation once the provider accepts your request. iOS coming soon.

Can I book appointments for my children or family members? ▾

Yes. Morava lets you add family members and dependents to your account. You can manage appointments for your entire family from a single account — no separate logins required.

Are Morava providers verified? ▾

All providers listed on Morava are sourced from public healthcare databases. We encourage patients to verify provider credentials independently through the Oklahoma Medical Board. We are actively onboarding providers who have claimed and verified their Morava profiles.
