Our Mission

Healthcare access shouldn't depend on your zip code.

In Oklahoma, over 800,000 residents rely on SoonerCare and Medicaid for their healthcare — yet finding a provider who accepts their insurance is frustratingly difficult.

Morava exists to fix that. We built the simplest, fastest way for Oklahoma patients to find quality care and book appointments — regardless of insurance status, location, or income.
