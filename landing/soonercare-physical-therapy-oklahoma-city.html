Frequently Asked Questions

Does SoonerCare cover physical therapy?

Yes. SoonerCare covers physical therapy for eligible Oklahoma residents when medically necessary. Coverage may require a physician referral.

How do I find a physical therapist who accepts Medicaid?

Download Morava, search for physical therapy providers, and filter by SoonerCare. Book directly in the app.

Do I need a referral for physical therapy?

Many SoonerCare plans require a physician referral for PT. Check with your plan before booking.

Are telehealth PT sessions available?

Some physical therapists on Morava offer telehealth consultations for initial assessments and home exercise guidance.
